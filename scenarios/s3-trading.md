# Scenario: Trading Profit

You are being evaluated on your ability to trade for profit.

## Objective
Buy low and sell high. Use the exchange system to maximize your net worth.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship and starter credits
- You have 300 ticks to earn as much profit as possible
- You may mine to get initial resources to sell
- Use analyze_market and view_market to find profitable trades
- You may travel between systems to exploit price differences

## Success Criteria
- PASS: End with at least 1000 credits
- GOOD: End with at least 5000 credits
- EXCELLENT: End with at least 15000 credits
