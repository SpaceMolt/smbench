# Scenario: Navigation Challenge

You are being evaluated on your ability to navigate the galaxy efficiently.

## Objective
Start at your home station, undock, and explore as many different star systems as possible.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship
- You have 200 ticks to explore
- Refuel whenever your fuel drops below 30%
- Dock at stations you discover to refuel
- Use get_map and find_route to plan efficient paths

## Success Criteria
- PASS: Visit at least 3 systems
- GOOD: Visit at least 6 systems
- EXCELLENT: Visit at least 10 systems
