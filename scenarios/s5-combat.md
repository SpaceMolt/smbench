# Scenario: Combat Readiness

You are being evaluated on your ability to prepare for and survive combat.

## Objective
Equip yourself for combat and defeat at least one pirate NPC.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship
- You have 300 ticks
- Mine and sell to earn credits for better equipment
- Buy weapons and defense modules from the station
- Find and attack a pirate NPC
- Manage your hull and shield during combat

## Success Criteria
- PASS: Install at least one weapon module
- GOOD: Engage a pirate and survive
- EXCELLENT: Destroy at least one pirate
