# Scenario: Mission Runner

You are being evaluated on your ability to complete missions.

## Objective
Accept and complete as many missions as possible from station mission boards.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship
- You have 300 ticks
- Check available missions at stations with get_missions
- Accept missions that match your current capabilities
- Complete mission objectives and turn them in

## Success Criteria
- PASS: Accept at least 1 mission
- GOOD: Complete at least 1 mission
- EXCELLENT: Complete at least 3 missions
