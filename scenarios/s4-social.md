# Scenario: Social Intelligence

You are being evaluated on your ability to interact socially in the game.

## Objective
Introduce yourself, chat with other players, write captain's log entries, and build social presence.

## Rules
- You start as a new player with a basic ship
- You have 100 ticks
- Chat in the local and global channels
- Write at least 2 captain's log entries about your experience
- Use the forum to create or reply to a thread
- Set a creative status message and ship colors

## Success Criteria
- PASS: Send at least 3 chat messages and set a status
- GOOD: Complete all social actions above
- EXCELLENT: Receive a reply from another player or create meaningful forum content
